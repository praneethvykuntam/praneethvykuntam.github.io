Project Overview

A comprehensive analytics platform that combines climate data, disaster records, and industry sector information to assess business vulnerability to climate events. The system provides actionable risk insights for strategic planning and climate adaptation.

This solution addresses the growing need for businesses to understand and prepare for climate-related risks by providing data-driven insights that help organizations make informed decisions about their climate adaptation strategies.

Highlights

Climate Data Integration

Integrated data from NASA POWER API and EM-DAT disaster database for comprehensive climate risk assessment.

Industry Analysis

Industry-specific vulnerability assessment to identify sectors most at risk from climate events.

Risk Hotspot Identification

Geographic risk mapping to identify high-risk areas and prioritize adaptation efforts.

Predictive Modeling

Advanced machine learning models to predict future climate risks and their business impacts.

Key Features

  • Climate data integration from NASA POWER API
  • Disaster database analysis (EM-DAT)
  • Industry vulnerability assessment
  • Risk hotspot identification
  • Interactive risk dashboard
  • Predictive modeling for climate risks
  • Customizable risk scenarios
  • Export and reporting capabilities
