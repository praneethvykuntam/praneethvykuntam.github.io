Analyzes government trade documents and news to predict policy changes and their economic impact on markets and businesses.

How It Works

1
Data Collection

Automatically collects trade policy documents, government announcements, and news articles from official sources and news APIs to build comprehensive datasets.

2
Text Processing

Uses NLP techniques to clean, tokenize, and extract key information from documents, identifying policy statements, trade terms, and economic indicators.

3
Sentiment Analysis

Applies machine learning models to classify policy sentiment and stance, determining whether policies are protectionist, liberalizing, or neutral.

4
Pattern Recognition

Identifies recurring patterns in policy language and timing to predict future policy directions and potential trade agreement changes.

5
Impact Modeling

Correlates policy changes with historical market movements to predict economic impacts on specific industries and currencies.

6
Alert Generation

Generates real-time alerts and reports when significant policy changes are detected, providing actionable insights for decision-makers.

Use Cases

Investment Strategy

Investment firms and hedge funds use policy predictions to adjust portfolios, hedge against trade risks, and identify market opportunities before policy changes impact prices.

Corporate Planning

Multinational corporations monitor trade policy changes to adjust supply chains, relocate operations, and develop contingency plans for policy-related disruptions.

Policy Research

Think tanks and research institutions use the platform to analyze policy trends, track government positions, and provide insights to policymakers and stakeholders.

Media Analysis

News organizations and journalists use policy predictions to provide timely coverage of trade developments and their potential economic implications.
