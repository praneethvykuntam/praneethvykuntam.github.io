Trade Policy Prediction

Using NLP to understand policy signals and forecast economic impacts

View on GitHub Back to Projects

Project Overview

This project uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze trade policy documents and predict their economic impacts. The system processes policy texts, extracts sentiment and key themes, and forecasts market movements based on policy signals.

By leveraging advanced NLP techniques and machine learning models, this solution provides valuable insights for policymakers, investors, and businesses to understand the potential economic implications of trade policy changes.

Highlights

Advanced NLP

Utilized transformer models and sentiment analysis to extract meaningful insights from policy documents.

Market Prediction

Developed models to predict market movements based on policy sentiment and key economic indicators.

Policy Timeline

Created comprehensive timeline visualization to track policy evolution and its market impact over time.

Real-time Analysis

Implemented real-time processing capabilities for continuous monitoring of policy changes and market reactions.

Key Features

  • Sentiment analysis of policy documents
  • Economic impact prediction models
  • Market movement forecasting
  • Policy timeline visualization
  • Text preprocessing and feature extraction
  • Multi-language support
  • Automated data collection
