Project Overview

An AI-powered semantic search and summarization engine designed for academic literature, initially focused on PubMed. The system helps researchers quickly discover and understand relevant papers through advanced NLP techniques and semantic understanding.

This solution addresses the challenge of information overload in academic research by providing intelligent search capabilities that understand context and meaning, not just keywords, making research more efficient and comprehensive.

Highlights

Semantic Search

Advanced semantic search capabilities that understand context and meaning beyond simple keyword matching.

Auto Summarization

Intelligent summarization of academic papers to quickly extract key insights and findings.

Citation Network

Visualization of citation networks to understand research relationships and influence patterns.

Topic Modeling

Advanced topic modeling and clustering to identify research trends and emerging areas of study.

Key Features

  • Semantic search across academic papers
  • Automatic paper summarization
  • Citation network analysis
  • Topic modeling and clustering
  • Interactive search interface
  • Multi-database support
  • Real-time search suggestions
  • Export and sharing capabilities
